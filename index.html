Use Text-to-Speech to Read Documents Aloud

Do you find you need to read the same document again and again before you can absorb the information?
Text-to-speech makes it easier to absorb information when you are reading.
You can see a quick example of text-to-speech here. You can use text to speech on your laptop, smartphone, or tablet. You can use text-to-speech to read out loud any kind of document: Powerpoint slides, PDFs, websites, Word documents, even books or handouts.

Use Grammarly to Proofread Your Essays and Assignments

I'm happy to confirm that you have been approved for using Grammarly as a support.
You can use Grammarly to check the spelling and grammar of your essays and assignments. It will also give you feedback on the type of mistakes you make and help you understand how to avoid those mistakes in future.

Getting Started

You can get started with Grammarly by clicking on this link.

Copy and paste the access code to activate Grammarly.

Using Grammarly

You can use Grammarly online, or you can use it with Microsoft Word by downloading the Grammarly Microsoft Word Add-in.
